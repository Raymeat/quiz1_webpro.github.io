KOTA TUA JAKARTA

Kota Tua, also known as Old Batavia, is a historic district that showcases Dutch colonial architecture, museums, and charming cafes. Walking through this area feels like stepping back in time, making it a must-visit for history lovers and photography enthusiasts.

RAGUNAN ZOO

Ragunan Zoo is one of the oldest and largest zoos in Indonesia, home to a wide variety of animals, including endangered species like orangutans and Komodo dragons. Surrounded by lush greenery, it’s a relaxing and educational destination for families.
